Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Invento (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Invento mobile application.

Information We Collect

Invento stores your data locally on your device. This includes product details, stock movements, categories, and images you choose to add.

We also use limited third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics – anonymous usage statistics and crash reports
  • Firebase Remote Config – app configuration settings
  • RevenueCat – subscription status and purchase management

We do not collect personally identifiable information, and we do not sell or share your data for advertising purposes.

Data Storage

All core app data is stored locally on your device.

Permissions

  • Camera – used only when you scan barcodes or take product photos
  • Photo Library – used only for images you manually select

Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple via the App Store and managed using RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment or billing details.

Your Rights

You control your data. You can delete your content at any time or remove all data by uninstalling the app.

Children’s Privacy

Invento is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
