Welcome to Swiper ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you use our mobile application.

By using Swiper, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal photos or data to any external servers.

üìä Information We Collect

Photo Library Access

Swiper requires access to your device's Photo Library to function. We request read and write permissions to:

  • Display your photos for review within the app
  • Allow you to delete photos you no longer want
  • Organize and manage your photo collection

Important: All photos remain on your device. We never upload, store, transmit, or access your photos on any external server or cloud service. Your photos are processed locally on your device only.

Usage Statistics

We collect minimal usage data stored locally on your device:

  • Number of photos deleted (for daily limit tracking)
  • Number of photos kept
  • Photo sorting preferences
  • Selected album filters

This data is stored locally using iOS UserDefaults and is never transmitted to external servers.

Notifications

Swiper may request permission to send you notifications. Notifications can be local (scheduled by the app on your device) or push (delivered via Apple's Push Notification service). We use notifications to:

  • Remind you to review and organize your photos
  • Inform you about feature updates or tips
  • Notify you about subscription status changes (e.g., renewal reminders)

Data and Permissions: If you enable notifications, your device may generate a push notification token. If used, this token is a device-specific identifier for delivering push notifications and does not contain personal information. We do not include your personal photos or their contents in notifications.

You can control notification permissions at any time in your device Settings. Disabling notifications will prevent future alerts but will not affect your ability to use the app.

Analytics and Diagnostics

To help us understand app performance and improve stability and features, we use Firebase services that may collect the following types of information:

  • Firebase Analytics: Aggregated usage information such as screen views, session duration, device model, OS version, and basic interaction events (e.g., button taps). We do not collect your personal photos or their contents.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash reports and diagnostics, which can include device model, OS version, app version, stack traces, and non-personal device identifiers to help us identify and fix issues.

These analytics and diagnostics are used solely to monitor app performance, troubleshoot problems, and guide product improvements. We do not use this data for advertising purposes.

Subscription Information

If you purchase a Premium subscription, subscription management is handled by RevenueCat, a third-party service. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Purchase transaction information
  • Subscription status and expiration dates
  • Device identifiers (for subscription validation)

We do not have access to your payment information, which is processed securely through Apple's App Store.

‚öôÔ∏è How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features like displaying photos, enabling deletion, and managing your photo library
  • Local Storage: To remember your preferences (sort order, selected album) for a better user experience
  • Subscription Management: To verify and manage your Premium subscription status through RevenueCat
  • Analytics: To understand how features are used and improve the app using aggregated, non-identifying information collected via Firebase Analytics

üíæ Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All data collected by Swiper is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This includes:

  • Usage statistics (deletion counts, kept photos)
  • App preferences (sort order, album selection)
  • Onboarding completion status

No Cloud Storage

We do not store any of your data on external servers or cloud services. Your photos, preferences, and usage data remain exclusively on your device.

Notification Tokens

If you opt in to push notifications, your device may generate a push notification token to enable delivery via Apple's Push Notification service (APNs). This token may be processed by our notification provider solely for the purpose of sending notifications and is not used to identify you personally. You can revoke notification permission at any time in Settings.

Third-Party Processing for Diagnostics and Analytics

While your photos and app preferences remain on your device, certain diagnostics and analytics data (such as crash reports and aggregated usage metrics) may be processed by Firebase on their servers to provide analytics and crash reporting services. This data does not include your personal photos.

Photos Remain on Device

Your photos are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere. They remain in your device's Photo Library, and when you delete photos through Swiper, they are deleted from your device's Photo Library only.

üîó Third-Party Services

Swiper uses the following third-party services:

Firebase (Analytics, Remote Config, Crashlytics)

We use Google Firebase to help us analyze app usage, manage remote configuration, and diagnose crashes. Firebase may collect device and app information such as device model, OS version, app version, coarse location (if provided by the OS), and identifiers used for analytics and crash reporting. We do not share your personal photos with Firebase.

  • Firebase Analytics: Provides aggregated usage data to help us understand feature usage and improve the app.
  • Firebase Remote Config: Allows us to remotely toggle or configure features and content without requiring an app update.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Provides crash reports and diagnostics to improve app stability.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy and the Firebase Privacy and Security documentation.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage Premium subscriptions. RevenueCat's privacy practices are governed by their own Privacy Policy. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Device identifiers for subscription validation
  • Purchase and subscription status information
  • App usage analytics (anonymized)

For more information, please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple App Store

Subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple handles all payment processing and may collect payment information in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not have access to your payment details.

Apple Push Notification service (APNs)

For push notifications, we use Apple's Push Notification service (APNs). APNs uses a device-specific token to route notifications to your device. This token does not reveal your identity or the contents of your photos. Notification delivery respects your system settings and preferences.

Apple Services

As an iOS app, Swiper operates within Apple's ecosystem and may use Apple's standard services (such as iCloud for app data backup if enabled by you). This is subject to Apple's privacy policies. Analytics collection respects your device privacy settings (for example, Limit Ad Tracking and system analytics settings).

üë§ Your Rights
