# Exif Remover: Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 23, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use **EXIF Remover**.

---

## Information We Collect

EXIF Remover is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function:

- Daily usage statistics (number of images processed) — stored locally on your device  
- Premium subscription status — managed through RevenueCat and Apple’s In-App Purchase system  
- **No personal information, images, or metadata are collected or transmitted**

**Important:**  
All image processing happens entirely on your device. Images are never uploaded to any server, and we have no access to your photos or their metadata.

---

## How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

- Track daily usage limits for free users  
- Manage premium subscription status  
- Improve app functionality  

---

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. This includes:

- Daily usage count  
- Last usage date  

This data never leaves your device and is automatically reset daily.  
If you delete the app, all stored data is permanently removed.

**Note:**  
We do not use cloud storage, analytics services, or any external data collection tools.

---

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service to provide premium subscription functionality:

### RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat processes subscription information through Apple’s secure payment system.

For more information about RevenueCat’s privacy practices, please visit:  
https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

---

## Photo Library Access

EXIF Remover requires access to your photo library to:

- Read images you select for processing  
- Save processed images to a dedicated album  

This access is granted only with your explicit permission through iOS system prompts.  
We never access your photos without your consent, and all processing happens locally on your device.

---

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Access:** You can view your daily usage statistics in the app  
- **Deletion:** You can reset your usage statistics at any time in Settings, or delete the app to remove all data  
- **Control:** You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings  

---

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.  
If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Any changes will be posted in the app and reflected by updating the **"Last updated"** date above.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store support page or via email if provided in the app description.
