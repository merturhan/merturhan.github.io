# Privacy Policy for Invento

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Introduction

Invento ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Invento (the "App").

By using Invento, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not download, install, or use the App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

When you use Invento, you may provide the following information:

- **Product Data**: Product names, prices, quantities, barcodes, product images, and categories you create
- **Stock Movements**: Records of inventory changes including movement type (in/out), quantities, reference numbers, notes, and timestamps
- **Category Information**: Category names and color preferences you assign
- **Product Images**: Photos you take or select from your device's photo library

All of this data is stored locally on your device using Core Data and never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up through iCloud or other backup services.

### Automatically Collected Information

We use third-party services that may automatically collect certain information:

- **Firebase Analytics**: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how you use the App, including:
  - App launches and session duration
  - Feature usage statistics
  - Crash reports and error logs
  - Device information (device type, operating system version, app version)
  
  This information is collected anonymously and aggregated. It does not identify you personally.

- **Firebase Remote Config**: We use Firebase Remote Config to deliver app configuration settings. This service does not collect personal information.

### Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Firebase** (Google): Used for analytics and remote configuration
- **RevenueCat**: Used to manage premium subscriptions and purchases through Apple's App Store

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
- Process premium subscription purchases and manage subscriptions
- Improve the App's performance and user experience
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
- Send you important updates about the App (if you opt in)
- Respond to your support requests

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

All your product data, stock movements, categories, and product images are stored locally on your device using Core Data. This includes:

- Product information (name, price, quantity, barcode, images)
- Category information (name, color)
- Stock movement history (type, quantity, reference numbers, notes, timestamps)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up through:
- iCloud Backup (if enabled on your device)
- iTunes or Finder backup
- Other backup services you configure

### Cloud Services

We use the following cloud services:

- **Firebase Analytics**: Collects anonymous usage statistics and crash reports
- **RevenueCat**: Manages subscription status and purchase history (processed according to Apple's privacy policies)
- **Firebase Remote Config**: Delivers app configuration settings without storing personal data

## Permissions

Invento requests the following permissions:

- **Camera Access**: Required to take photos of products and scan barcodes. Camera access is only used when you actively use these features. We do not access your camera in the background.

- **Photo Library Access**: Required to select product images from your device's photo library. We only access photos you explicitly choose to add to products.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device's Settings. Some features may not work if permissions are revoked.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With trusted third-party services (Firebase, RevenueCat) that help us operate the App. These services are bound by their own privacy policies and terms of service.

- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.

- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- Local data is encrypted using iOS security features and stored securely on your device
- Secure connections (HTTPS) for all network communications
- Regular security updates and patches
- Access controls and authentication for third-party services

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access Your Data**: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible through the App
- **Delete Your Data**: You can delete your data by uninstalling the App or deleting individual products, categories, or movements
- **Opt Out of Analytics**: You can disable analytics by turning off analytics in your device settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements)
- **Revoke Permissions**: You can revoke camera or photo library access at any time through your device's Settings
- **Request Information**: You can contact us to request information about data we collect

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

## Premium Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are managed through RevenueCat and Apple's App Store. When you purchase a subscription:

- Payment is processed by Apple according to Apple's terms and privacy policy
- Subscription information (status, purchase history) is managed by RevenueCat
- We do not have access to your payment information or credit card details
- Subscription data is used solely to provide premium features within the App

You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings:
- Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions

## International Users

If you are using the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of information to the United States.
