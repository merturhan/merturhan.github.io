# Support - Invento

Thank you for using Invento! We're here to help you get the most out of your inventory management experience.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**How do I add my first product?**
1. Open Invento and tap the "Products" tab at the bottom
2. Tap the "+" button in the top right corner
3. Enter product details:
   - Product name (required)
   - Price (optional)
   - Quantity (optional)
   - Barcode (optional - can be scanned or entered manually)
4. Optionally add a category, product image, or other details
5. Tap "Save" to add the product

**How do I scan a barcode?**
1. Go to the "Scanner" tab
2. Tap "Tap to Scan"
3. Grant camera permission if prompted (first time only)
4. Position the barcode within the frame on your screen
5. The app will automatically detect and process the barcode
6. If the product exists, you can update its quantity
7. If the product doesn't exist, you'll be prompted to create a new product

**How do I create categories?**
1. When adding or editing a product, tap on "Category"
2. Tap "Manage Categories"
3. Tap "New Category" or the "+" button
4. Enter a category name
5. Choose a color for the category
6. Tap "Done" to save

**How do I add a product image?**
1. When adding or editing a product, scroll to "Product Image" section
2. Tap "Add Image" or "Change Image"
3. Choose "Take Photo" to use your camera, or "Choose from Library" to select an existing photo
4. Grant camera or photo library permission if prompted (first time only)
5. The image will be saved with your product

### Stock Management

**How do I record stock movements?**
1. Go to the "History" tab
2. Select a date using the date picker at the top
3. Tap the "+" button to add a movement
4. Choose a product from the list
5. Select movement type: "In" (stock added) or "Out" (stock removed)
6. Enter the quantity
7. Optionally add a reference number and note
8. Tap "Save" to record the movement

**What are low stock alerts?**
The Dashboard automatically identifies products with low quantities (less than 5 units) and displays them in the "Low Stock Alert" section. You can tap on any alert to view and update the product details.

**How do I update product quantity?**
There are several ways to update product quantity:
- **Quick Update**: Use the Scanner tab to scan a barcode and quickly adjust quantity
- **Manual Update**: Go to Products tab, tap on a product, edit the quantity field, and save
- **Stock Movement**: Record a stock movement in the History tab

**How do I delete a product?**
1. Go to the "Products" tab
2. Swipe left on the product you want to delete
3. Tap "Delete"
4. Confirm the deletion

**Can I restore a deleted product?**
No, deleted products cannot be restored. Make sure to back up your data regularly using iCloud or iTunes/Finder backup.

### Premium Features

**What is included in Premium?**
Premium subscription includes:
- Unlimited products (Free version has product limits that can be configured)
- Advanced analytics and insights
- Priority support
- All premium features unlocked

**How do I upgrade to Premium?**
1. When you reach the product limit, tap the "Premium" button
2. Or look for Premium options throughout the app
3. Choose your subscription plan:
   - Monthly subscription
   - Yearly subscription (better value)
4. Complete the purchase through Apple's App Store
5. Your premium features will be activated immediately

**How do I restore my Premium purchase?**
If you've previously purchased Premium on another device or after reinstalling the app:
1. Contact support at merturhan2k@proton.me with your Apple ID email
2. We can help restore your subscription
3. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase

**Can I cancel my subscription?**
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings:
1. Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
2. Tap [Your Name] at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Find "Invento" in the list
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

**Will I lose Premium features immediately after canceling?**
No, you'll continue to have access to Premium features until the end of your current billing period. After that, you'll revert to the free version.

**What happens to my data if I cancel Premium?**
All your data remains intact. You'll still have access to all products you've added, but you may be limited by the free version's product limit for adding new products.

### Troubleshooting

**The app crashes or freezes**
- Force close the app: Swipe up from the bottom (or double-tap home button on older devices) and swipe up on the Invento app
- Restart your device
- Make sure you're running the latest version of the app from the App Store
- Check that you have sufficient storage space available
- If the problem persists, contact support with details about when the crash occurs

**Barcode scanner not working**
- Make sure you've granted camera permissions:
  - Go to Settings > Invento > Camera
  - Ensure "Camera" is enabled
- Check that you're in a well-lit area
- Ensure the barcode is clear, not damaged, and fully visible
- Try cleaning your camera lens
- Make sure you're holding the device steady
- Try scanning from different distances

**Camera permission denied**
- Go to Settings > Invento > Camera
- Toggle the switch to enable camera access
- Restart the app

**Photo library permission denied**
- Go to Settings > Invento > Photos
- Select "All Photos" or "Selected Photos"
- Restart the app

**Data not saving**
- Check that you have sufficient storage space on your device
- Make sure you're not in airplane mode (for sync features)
- Try force closing and reopening the app
- Restart your device
- If the problem persists, contact support

**Can't find a product**
- Use the search bar at the top of the Products tab
- Check if you've accidentally filtered by category
- Make sure the product name or barcode matches your search term
- Try searching with partial names or barcodes

**Products disappeared**
- Check if you accidentally deleted them
- Verify you're looking at the correct date in History tab
- Check if you have any active filters applied
- If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your data should be backed up

**App is slow or laggy**
- Close other apps running in the background
- Restart your device
- Make sure you have sufficient storage space (at least 1GB free)
- If you have many products with images, consider removing some images to improve performance

**Can't add more products (Free version)**
- You've reached the product limit for free users
- Upgrade to Premium for unlimited products
- Or delete some existing products to make room

### Data and Privacy

**Where is my data stored?**
All your product data, stock movements, categories, and images are stored locally on your device using Core Data. We use Firebase Analytics for anonymous usage statistics, but your inventory data never leaves your device.

**Can I backup my data?**
Yes, your data is automatically backed up if you have iCloud Backup enabled:
- Go to Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > iCloud Backup
- Make sure "iCloud Backup" is enabled
- Your data will be backed up automatically when your device is charging and connected to Wi-Fi

You can also manually back up through:
- iTunes (on Windows or older Macs)
- Finder (on macOS Catalina and later)

**How do I delete all my data?**
Uninstalling the app will remove all local data. To delete all data:
1. Delete all products manually, or
2. Uninstall the app from your device

**Important**: Make sure to back up important data before uninstalling, as this action cannot be undone.

**Is my data secure?**
Yes, your data is stored securely on your device using iOS encryption. We do not have access to your inventory data. All data transmission uses secure HTTPS connections.

**Do you collect personal information?**
We only collect anonymous usage statistics through Firebase Analytics (such as app launches and feature usage). We do not collect personally identifiable information. Your inventory data stays on your device.

**Can I export my data?**
Currently, there is no built-in export feature. Your data is stored locally and can be backed up through iCloud or iTunes/Finder. If you need to export your data, please contact support.

### Features and Tips

**Dashboard Analytics**
- View your total inventory value, product count, categories, and low stock alerts
- Switch between different chart types:
  - Value Distribution: See how your inventory value changes over time
  - Quantity Trends: Track quantity changes
  - Category Distribution: View how your inventory is distributed across categories
  - Product Activity: See product movement activity over time
- Change time ranges (Week/Month/Year) to see different periods
- Tap on charts for detailed information
- Pull down to refresh data

**Quick Stock Updates**
- Use the Scanner tab for the fastest way to update inventory
- Scan a barcode to instantly find and update product quantities
- Recent scans are saved for quick access
- You can quickly add or remove stock directly from the scanner

**Product Images**
- Add photos to products for easy visual identification
- Take photos directly in the app or choose from your library
- Images are stored locally on your device
- Product images help you quickly identify products in your inventory list

**Category Organization**
- Use color-coded categories to visually organize products
- View category distribution in Dashboard analytics
- Filter products by category in the Products tab
- Create as many categories as you need

**Stock Movement History**
- View complete history of all stock movements
- Filter by date, movement type (in/out), or product
- Add reference numbers and notes to track specific transactions
- Use history to audit inventory changes

**Search and Sort**
- Search products by name or barcode
- Sort products by:
  - Name (A-Z or Z-A)
  - Price (Low to High or High to Low)
  - Quantity (Low to High or High to Low)
  - Last Updated (Newest or Oldest)

**Low Stock Management**
- Dashboard automatically shows products with low stock
- Set your own low stock threshold by monitoring quantities
- Use alerts to know when to reorder products
