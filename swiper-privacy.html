Getting Started

Welcome to Swiper! Here's how to get the most out of your photo organization experience:

1 Grant Photo Access

When you first open Swiper, you'll be asked to grant access to your photo library. This is necessary for the app to display and organize your photos.

2 Start Swiping

Swipe right on photos you want to keep, and swipe left on photos you want to delete. The app will mark photos for deletion but won't delete them immediately.

3 Review and Delete

When you're ready, tap the "Finish" button to review all marked photos and delete them in bulk. This helps prevent accidental deletions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete photos?

Swipe left on photos you want to delete. They'll be marked for deletion. When you're ready, tap the "Finish" button to delete all marked photos at once.

Can I undo a deletion?

Unfortunately, once photos are deleted, they cannot be recovered through the app. We recommend reviewing your selections carefully before confirming deletion.

What is the daily deletion limit?

Free users can delete up to 25 photos per day. Premium users have unlimited deletions. You can upgrade to Premium in the Settings tab.

Troubleshooting

Photos not loading

  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Make sure you have photos in your library

App crashes or freezes

  • Update to the latest version of the app
  • Restart your device
  • If the problem persists, contact support

Can't delete photos

  • Check if you've reached your daily deletion limit (25 for free users)
  • Make sure you have write permissions for your photo library
  • Try selecting fewer photos at once
