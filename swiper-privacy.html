Swiper - Your Privacy Matters

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Swiper ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

By using Swiper, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Key Principle: Swiper processes all photos locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos to any external servers.

Information We Collect

Photo Library Access

Swiper requires access to your photo library to display and organize your photos. This access is granted through iOS permissions and is used solely for the app's functionality.

  • Local Processing Only: All photo operations (viewing, organizing, deleting) occur entirely on your device.
  • No Upload: We do not upload your photos to any server or cloud service.
  • No Storage: We do not store copies of your photos.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app:

  • App version and device information
  • Feature usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Crash reports and error logs

This data is collected through standard iOS analytics and does not include personal information or photo content.

Premium Subscription Data

If you purchase a Premium subscription, we process payment information through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide photo organization features
  • Improvement: To analyze app performance and improve user experience
  • Support: To respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage Premium subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect certain information as described in their privacy policy. We recommend reviewing their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Apple App Store

Premium subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple's privacy policy applies to payment transactions.

Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics services to understand app usage patterns. These services do not have access to your photos or personal information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All photo processing occurs locally on your device
  • No network transmission of photo data
  • Secure handling of subscription information through Apple's secure payment system
  • Regular security updates and patches

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at merturhan2k@proton.me.

Children's Privacy

Swiper is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Photos: Never stored by us - all processing is local
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years for analytics purposes
  • Support Communications: Retained for up to 3 years

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

International Data Transfers

Your data is processed on your device and may be stored in your region. We do not transfer your photo data internationally. Any analytics data may be processed by third-party services in various jurisdictions, always in compliance with applicable data protection laws.
